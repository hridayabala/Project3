In this website, you can learn about different types of cancer.

Skin Cancer

Skin cancer is the one of the most common cancers in the United States. To lower your skin cancer risk, protect your skin from the sun and avoid indoor tanning. The survival rate of a person with skin cancer depends on their age and their access to care and treatment.

Breast Cancer

Breast cancer is the most common cancer around the world. It is normally found to be diagnosed in older women. Some ways to prevent breast cancer is by keeping a healthy weight, being physically active, and either choosing not to drink alcohol, or drinking it in moderation.

Lung Cancer

Lung cancer is a common type of cancer, that can be cured if found in its early stages. It is mostly caused by smoking, secondhand smoke, or exposure to chemcicals and toxins. Symptoms include cough, chest pain, wheezing, and weight loss, but they don't appear until the cancer is advanced.

Bone Cancer

Bone cancer can begin in any bone in the body, but it most commonly affects the pelvis or the long bones in the arms and legs. A bone tumor may cause a painless mass. Some people have dull, aching pain. And in some cases, minor injury causes a fracture near the tumor. Bone cancer is rare, making up less than 1 percent of all cancers.

Leukemia

Leukemia is a broad term for cancers of the blood cells. The type of leukemia depends on the type of blood cell that becomes cancer and whether it grows quickly or slowly. Leukemia occurs most often in adults older than 55, but it is also the most common cancer in children younger than 15.

Pancreatic Cancer

Pancreatic cancer begins in the tissues of your pancreas — an organ in your abdomen that lies behind the lower part of your stomach. This type of cancer is often detected late, spreads rapidly, and has a poor prognosis. There are no symptoms in the early stages. Gastric Cancer

Gastric cancer is a disease in which malignant (cancer) cells form in the lining of the stomach. Age, diet, and stomach disease can affect the risk of developing gastric cancer. Symptoms of gastric cancer include indigestion and stomach discomfort or pain.

Kidney Cancer

Kidney cancer develops when cells in your kidneys change and grow out of control. People with kidney cancer may notice flank pain, high blood pressure, blood in their pee and other symptoms. Kidney cancer treatments include surgery, chemotherapy and radiation therapy.

Head & Neck Cancer

A group of cancers of the mouth, sinuses, nose, or throat.To lower your risk for head and neck cancers, don’t use tobacco products, limit the amount of alcohol you drink, avoid indoor tanning, and talk to your doctor about HPV vaccination. Smoking is a significant risk factor.
